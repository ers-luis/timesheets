Easily manage your timesheets for EU Projects

This is an App to control your time spent on EU-Project-related activities.

Manifest:

  • This app belongs to ERS
  • ERS controls and provides access to the app
  • All clients of ERS are offered free use of this tool


Project goals:

  • Manage your work in institutions and EU projects
  • Keep track of the time spent on your activities
  • Manage the institutions you work with
  • Manage the Projects you work in
  • Add Projects, WP and activities to your profile and keep track of the time spent on them
